Upcoming Tournament

League of Legends Championship 2025

May 20–25, 2025

The League of Legends Championship 2025 is the biggest Esports event in Southeast Asia, featuring 16 top teams competing for a total prize pool of 500 million VND. The tournament will follow a double elimination format, with Bo3 matches, and Bo5 for semifinals and finals, promising thrilling battles. Alongside the main tournament, a fan zone will host cosplay, interactive mini-games, and booths showcasing the latest gaming gear. The event will be live-streamed on Twitch and YouTube with Vietnamese and English commentary. Free entry at TD Arena, Ho Chi Minh City.
